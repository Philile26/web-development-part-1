# website for Soweto Transit Solutions/ Soweto Commuter

This website is created for the Soweto Commuter :
primary Audience : Residents of Soweto who use taxis daily for school,work and shopping.
secondary Audience : Tourists who unfamiliar with the rank systems like Bara or Maponya who  need a reliable guide to navigate the township safely.
stake holders: Taxi owners and drivers  who need a formal platform to list their services and communicate with the public.

The website is created to bridge the gap between the informal taxi industry and digital accessibility . 
Expect this information in website:
Professionalise the image of Soweto taxi associations.
Improve tansparency regarding fares and routes.
Enhance safety by providing direct channels for feeback and emergency imformation.
