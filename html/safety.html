Commuter Rights

Every passenger in a Soweto taxi has the right to a safe journey. If you feel unsafe, use the tools below:

  • The "Front Seat" Rule: Ensure the driver is not using a mobile phone while driving.
  • Overloading: Do not board a taxi that exceeds its legal capacity (15+1).
  • Panic Button: Call our hotline at 0800-TAXI-SAFE for immediate assistance.
