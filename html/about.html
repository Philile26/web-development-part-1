Our History

Soweto Transit Solutions emerged from the late 70s when township entrepreneurs stepped into the vaccum created by an unwillingness to invest in safe , affordable public transport for majority of workers. Starting as a small association of local drivers in Orlando , we have grown into a coordinated network that oversees thousands of daily trips between Soweto and Johannesburg CBD.

Our Mission

To provide safe, reliable, and affordable public transport solutions that connect the Soweto community to the economic opportunities of Johannesburg CBD through innovative solutions like digital ticket sales, booking options and real time fare updates. We aim to make commuting easy and accessible for everyone

Meet our Leadership Team

Our board consists of industry veterans and community leaders who are passionate about improving the daily commute for Soweto residents. From our CEO, who has over 20 years of experience in the transport sector, to our safety officer who is a former traffic cop, our team is dedicated to ensuring that every trip is a safe and pleasant experience for our passengers.
