1. Daily Commuter Transport

Our primary service involves high-frequency trips between Soweto residential areas and major economic hubs.

  • Local Routes: Internal Soweto travel (e.g., Jabulani to Meadowlands).
  • Long Distance: Daily trips to Johannesburg CBD, Sandton, and Randburg.
  • Student Shuttles: Dedicated morning and afternoon routes for local schools.

2. Private Charter & Events

Need a dedicated vehicle for a group? We provide reliable charter services for local events.

Funeral & Church Bookings

Respectful and punctual group transport for local congregations and family gatherings.

Sporting Events

Direct transport to FNB Stadium or Orlando Stadium for soccer matches and concerts.

3. Local Courier & Parcels

Utilizing our frequent routes to move small parcels across the township quickly.

How it works: Drop your parcel at a registered rank office, and it will be delivered to the destination rank for collection within hours.
